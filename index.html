Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

The people's president

The life and achievements of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam:

  • 1931: A great scientist born.
  • 1960: Becomes a scientist at the Defence Research and Development Organisation(DRDO),India.
  • 1969: Joins the space research at the Indian Space Research Organisation(ISRO).
  • 1980: Becomes the project director of India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III).
  • 1981: Receives the Padma Bhushan for his incredible work at the ISRO.
  • 1990: Receives the Padma Vibhushan for his works at the ISRO and the DRDO.
  • 1997: Receives the Bharat Ratna,the highest civilian award in India.
  • 2002: Becomes the 11th President of India.
  • 2007: End of his Presidency.
  • 2015: The People's President passes away.
